Build Your Fish Tank

Select the type of fish tank you want to build. Choose between Saltwater fish tank or Freshwater fish tank:

Saltwater Fish Tank

Saltwater tanks are ideal for keeping marine fish and corals. Below are some fish that thrive in saltwater conditions:

  • Clownfish: A popular choice for saltwater tanks, known for their bright colors.
  • Blue Tang: A vibrant blue fish that adds beauty to any tank.
  • Yellow Tang: Known for its striking yellow color and active nature.
  • Mandarinfish: Known for its vibrant patterns and unique appearance.
  • Royal Gramma: A small fish with bright purple and yellow coloration.

Freshwater Fish Tank

Freshwater tanks are easier to maintain and are perfect for beginners. Here are some fish that thrive in freshwater conditions:

  • Betta Fish: Known for their colorful fins and relatively easy care.
  • Neon Tetra: Small, colorful fish that are perfect for community tanks.
  • Guppy: Hardy fish with a wide range of colors and patterns.
  • Corydoras: Small, bottom-dwelling fish known for their peaceful nature.
  • Swordtail: Recognizable by its long, sword-like tail and vibrant colors.
